Bachelor of Pharmacy ( B. Pharma) is a four-year undergraduate program in which students study the methods and process of preparing drugs .

Bachelor of Technology

A Bachelor of Technology is an undergraduate academic degree conferred after the completion of a three-year, a four-year or a five-year program .

ITIs or Industrial Training Institutes are usually organisations or training centres that provide courses to students seeking easy employment just after completing school.
